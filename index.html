About Us

Riverbend Medieval Society Inc. is a vibrant community dedicated to bringing medieval history to life through authentic re-enactment. We offer a unique opportunity to step back in time and experience the rich culture, traditions, and crafts of the medieval period.

Join Our Society

Are you interested in a new and exciting hobby? Looking to immerse yourself in medieval history and culture? Riverbend Medieval Society Inc. welcomes new members who share our passion for historical re-enactment.

Our 2025 season promises to be filled with exciting events, workshops, and demonstrations. Don't miss out on this opportunity to be part of our growing community!
